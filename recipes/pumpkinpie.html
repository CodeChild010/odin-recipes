Pumpkin Pie

Recipe

A cake in Minecraft is "crafted" by gathering the following: 3x wheat, 3x buckets of milk, 2x sugar, and 1 egg. Place your ingredients in a crafting table in the assortment displayed above.

Ingredients

  • 3x Wheat
  • 3x Buckets of Milk
  • 2x Sugar
  • 1x Egg

Steps

  1. Open crafting table grid
  2. Place 3 buckets of milk across the top row
  3. Place 3 wheat across the bottom row
  4. Place egg in center grid
  5. Place sugar in remaining empty slots in crafting grid
